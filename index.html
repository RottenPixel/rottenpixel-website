About Crimson Wanderer

Video Gameplay

Join the adventure!

What starts as a simple job quickly turns into a dangerous adventure in pursuit of a legendary treasure.

Join treasure hunters Lou and Siva on a race against time to find the mythical Chariot, while trying to escape an island ruled by ruthless mercenaries and decorated tyrants.

The Arcade is back... with a twist

Blast your way through a thrilling adventure that brings back the spirit of 90s arcade classics… with a modern twist.

Explore a world of interconnected levels filled with secrets, challenges, and alternate paths. Upgrade your multi-ballistic weapon, unlock powerful new shot types, and learn new abilities as you uncover the mysteries of a dangerous and enigmatic island in a fast-paced, vibrant 2D experience.

Handcrafter 2D Glory

Immerse yourself in a colorful, high-definition visual experience, entirely hand-drawn with pencil on paper and packed with detail.

Straight to the Action

No filler. No endless dialogue. No convoluted lore.

Take on challenging platforming sections, escape deadly ambushes, face waves of enemies, make exciting discoveries, and test your skills against fearsome bosses.

And this is only the beginning!

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Crimson Wanderer have a publisher?

Not at the moment—we’ve been developing Crimson Wanderer on our own so far. But we’d love for a publisher to get in touch with us.

Are there plans for a full version of Crimson Wanderer?

We’d love to! However, at the moment we can’t afford to develop a full version on our own. We’ll be launching our Kickstarter campaign soon so that you can help us make the full version of Crimson Wanderer a reality.

Is there a simple way to help with the development?

Of course! Look for our game on Steam – Crimson Wanderer – and add it to your wishlist. That’ll prove there’s interest in the game and help spread the word to more people.
